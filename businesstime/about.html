About

We are a small industrial maintance located in east Tennessee. When repairs are necessary our aim is to repair equipment on site to minimize your cost and down-time.

However, before repairs are necessary we can work with you to setup predictivie maintance plans to aid in your care. Even with preventative maintence, stuff eventually breaks. With predictive maintence we can monitor your machinery's "health" and schedule repairs at your convience as a means to prevent unexpected and more costly down-time.

Our service area includes middle Tennessee, north Georgia, and eastern Kentucky
